## What is it?

When you capture a photo for your road report, our AI automatically detects and blurs any faces in the image to protect everyone's privacy.

## How does it work?

1. **Take a photo** using the camera button in the report form
2. **AI analyzes** the image in real-time (takes 1-2 seconds)
3. **Faces are blurred** automatically - you don't need to do anything!
4. **Submit your report** as normal

## FAQs

### Q: Can I disable face blurring?
**A**: No. This feature is always on to protect everyone's privacy.

### Q: What if I want to show a person?
**A**: For safety reports, it's best to focus on the road condition, not people. If you need to report someone's actions, describe it in text.

### Q: Does this work on my phone?
**A**: Yes! Works on all modern smartphones (iPhone, Android) using Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge.

### Q: Does it use my data/internet?
**A**: The AI model downloads once (1MB) and is cached. After that, it works offline.

### Q: Is it accurate?
**A**: Yes! 95%+ accuracy for frontal faces. May miss extreme angles or obstructed faces.

### Q: What if someone's face isn't blurred?
**A**: Contact support and we'll manually blur it. The AI is very good but not perfect.
